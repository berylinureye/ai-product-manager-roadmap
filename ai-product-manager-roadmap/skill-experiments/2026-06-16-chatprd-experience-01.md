# ChatPRD Experience 01 - AI PM Research Agent PRD

Date: 2026-06-16

Tool: [ChatPRD](https://www.chatprd.ai/)

Plan used: Free

Test prompt:

```text
我正在转向 AI 产品经理，想做一个作品集项目：AI PM Research Agent。

请帮我写一份 PRD。这个产品的目标是帮助 AI 产品经理或转行者快速分析一个 AI 产品、GitHub 项目或行业方向，并输出结构化产品分析、用户细分、价值主张、MVP 方案、风险假设和作品集角度。

请包含：
1. 背景和问题
2. 目标用户
3. 用户痛点
4. 核心场景
5. MVP 功能
6. 非 MVP 范围
7. 成功指标
8. 风险和假设
9. 后续迭代方向
```

## ChatPRD Output

### TL;DR

AI PM Research Agent is a research and analysis tool designed for current or aspiring AI Product Managers. It enables rapid, structured analysis of AI products, open-source projects, or industry trends by generating actionable insights such as user segmentation, value propositions, MVP design, and portfolio-ready output. The tool targets PMs, career changers, and product analysts aiming to stay competitive in the AI field.

### 1. Background and Problem

The rapid evolution of the AI industry demands that PMs quickly grasp new concepts, assess products, and distill opportunities. Traditional research is slow, unstructured, and often misses actionable insights. For AI PMs, evaluating repositories or trends and producing professional-grade analysis for personal growth or portfolio use is a constant pain point.

### 2. Target Users

- Current AI Product Managers
- Aspiring PMs seeking to break into the AI domain
- Product analysts and UX researchers focusing on AI
- Students and career changers building a PM portfolio

### 3. User Pain Points

- Time-consuming research process for AI products or tech
- Difficulty in generating structured, actionable insights
- Lack of expertise/context about new AI domains
- Need for portfolio-ready artifacts and clear, concise analyses
- Fragmented understanding due to scattered information sources

### 4. Core Scenarios

- User inputs an AI product, GitHub repo, or keyword trend
- Agent analyzes the product and outputs a structured report, including:
  - Product overview
  - User segmentation
  - Value proposition and differentiation
  - MVP proposal
  - Risk assessment
  - Portfolio usage suggestions
- User reviews, edits, and exports the report for job interviews or case studies

### 5. MVP Features

- Input method for product/repo/trend (URL, keyword, text)
- Automated structured report generation covering all key analysis blocks
- Editable report interface with saving/export (PDF/Markdown)
- User guidance/wizard for first-time users
- Portfolio-focused output option (tone, tips, formatting)

### 6. Out of MVP Scope (Non-MVP)

- Real-time collaboration or sharing within tool
- Deep integration with 3rd-party PM tools (Jira, Notion)
- Advanced AI summarization (video demos, code reviews)
- Long-term storage or version history

### 7. Success Metrics

User-centric metrics:

- Number of reports generated per user
- User satisfaction (survey NPS, qualitative feedback)
- Repeat usage rate

Business metrics:

- Total active users (weekly/monthly)
- Conversion to paid/export features (if applicable)

Technical metrics:

- Report generation time (<2min)
- Export reliability (error rate <2%)

Tracking plan:

- Report generation event
- Export/copy event
- Feature usage breakdown

### 8. Risks and Assumptions

Risks:

- Data quality or relevance of AI product analysis
- Over-reliance on automation missing critical nuance
- Users' portfolio needs may be more niche than expected

Assumptions:

- Users are motivated to learn by example
- PM research frameworks can be automated at least to a first draft
- Career changers value structured analysis more than deep technical deep-dives

### 9. Future Directions

- Integrated AI/PM learning modules
- Support for collaborative analysis, peer review
- Integration with PM workflows (Jira, Notion, Confluence)
- Data-enriched analysis (benchmarks, competitor datasets)
- Personal knowledge base for ongoing learning and progress tracking

## Chinese Translation

AI PM Research Agent 是一个面向现任或准 AI 产品经理的研究分析工具。它可以帮助用户快速、结构化地分析 AI 产品、开源项目或行业趋势，并生成可执行的洞察，例如用户细分、价值主张、MVP 设计，以及可用于作品集的输出内容。目标用户包括产品经理、转行者和希望保持 AI 竞争力的产品分析人员。

### 1. 背景和问题

AI 行业变化很快，产品经理需要快速理解新概念、评估产品，并提炼机会点。传统研究过程慢、不够结构化，而且经常停留在信息整理，缺少可执行洞察。

对于 AI 产品经理来说，评估一个 GitHub 仓库、AI 产品或行业趋势，并产出专业级分析，用于个人成长或作品集，是一个持续存在的痛点。

### 2. 目标用户

- 现任 AI 产品经理
- 希望进入 AI 领域的准产品经理
- 关注 AI 的产品分析师和 UX 研究员

### 3. 用户痛点

- 研究 AI 产品或技术很耗时间
- 难以生成结构化、可执行的洞察
- 对新 AI 领域缺少背景和判断框架
- 需要可用于作品集的分析成果
- 信息来源分散，理解容易碎片化

### 4. 核心场景

- 用户输入一个 AI 产品、GitHub 仓库或趋势关键词

- 面向首次用户的引导流程

### 6. 非 MVP 范围

- 实时协作或工具内分享
- 与第三方 PM 工具深度集成，例如 Jira、Notion

用户指标：

埋点计划：

- 报告生成事件

- 相比深度技术分析，转行者更重视结构化分析能力

## Product Review

### What Worked

- The structure is complete and easy to read.
- It covers the requested sections without missing major PRD blocks.
- It is good enough as a PRD v0 draft.
- The language is clean and could be shared with a team after light editing.

### What Was Weak

- The answer is only in English, which makes it less convenient for Chinese learning notes and GitHub reflection.
- It is generic. It reads like a template-filled PRD rather than a deeply reasoned product decision.
- It does not proactively ask clarifying questions about the target user, source quality, data source, or output depth.
- It lacks competitor awareness. It does not compare against ChatPRD itself, `pm-skills`, Perplexity, NotebookLM, Claude, or ChatGPT.
- The MVP is still phrased as "build a tool" instead of "run the smallest validation experiment."
- Success metrics are standard SaaS metrics, but they do not directly measure whether the tool helps career switchers build stronger portfolios.
- It misses AI-specific product risks such as hallucination, citation quality, bad cases, fallback, and source confidence.

### Initial Judgment

ChatPRD is useful for producing a clean first PRD draft quickly. However, in this test it behaved more like a document generator with PM templates than a deep research partner.

For this project, ChatPRD is best treated as:

> A fast PRD drafting competitor, not the final benchmark for AI PM research judgment.

### One-Sentence Takeaway

ChatPRD's first PRD output is structurally complete but template-like; it is useful as a v0 draft, but a portfolio-quality artifact still needs stronger competitor analysis, validation design, AI risk handling, and career-switcher-specific differentiation.
